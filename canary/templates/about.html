About Canary

Canary is an application for publishing and managing cryptographically-verified warrant canary-like statements on a regular schedule.

A canary is PGP-signed message that is republished on a regular basis. Canary allows you to easily share with others how often to expect your canary to be republished, and makes it easy for you to republish your canary on schedule by sending you periodic reminder emails. Others can also watch your canary so they'll receive an alert when it's overdue, republished, or deleted entirely.

Warrant Canaries

Canary operates under the notion that while governments compel silence by issuing gag orders, it's much more difficult to compel speech, especially false speech.

From canarywatch.org:

A warrant canary is a colloquial term for a regularly published statement that a service provider has not received legal process (like a national security letter) that it would be prohibited from disclosing to the public. Once a service provider does receive legal process, the speech prohibition goes into place, and the provider no longer makes the statement about the number of such process received.
